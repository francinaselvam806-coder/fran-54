How GigFinder Works

We connect local neighbors who need help with verified students looking to earn money. It's safe, simple, and supports your community.

home

For Neighbors

01

Post a Service Request

Describe what you need help with using our AI Assistant or browse available student services. From dog walking to tech support, help is just a click away.

02

Choose a Verified Student

Review profiles, ratings, and IDs. All students on GigFinder are ID-verified for your peace of mind. Chat directly to discuss details.

03

Get it Done

The student completes the task. You pay them directly or through the platform. Rate their service to help build their professional reputation.

school

For Students

01

Create Your Profile

Sign up with your student email and verify your ID. Showcase your skills, from tutoring to gardening, to stand out to neighbors.

02

Browse or Post Gigs

Apply for open requests in your area or enable "Open to Work" to let neighbors find you. Set your own fair prices.

03

Earn & Build Rep

Complete tasks, earn money for tuition or expenses, and gather 5-star reviews to build a resume of trust and reliability.

shield

Safety First, Always.

Every student on GigFinder goes through a mandatory ID check. We prioritize safety so you can focus on community.
